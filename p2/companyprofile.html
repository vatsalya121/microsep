COMPANY PROFILE

Micro Seprations

Micro Separations is a client oriented manufacturing company specialized in the manufacturing and supplying of a wide range of products, including Micro Glass Fiber Filters, Micro Glass Fiber Thimbles, Cellulose Extraction Thimbles and PTFE Filters. We are a prominent manufacturer of the products, We offer different types of products that are widely demanded by various laboratories & industrial applications. We have expertise in offering qualitative range of PTFE Membrane to the clients spread across the country.

Complementary to the standard products, we provide superior quality products that ensure optimum performance. With quality products on offer, we are able to successfully fulfill the needs of relatively large volume and highly varied product requirements of the customer. Moreover, our flexible approach helps us in offering the clients with desired range, residing across the nation. These products can be availed at market leading prices. Established in the year 2012, Micro Separations has carved a distinct position as a prime Supplier of a wide range of products in a very short time of our existence
